Description:
Dynamic Financial Risk Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard that visualizes key financial metrics, including total revenue, average revenue, and product rankings across regions. It helps analyze financial risks with year-wise and quarter-wise data trends.

Technologies:

Power BI for data visualization
Dynamic filters for time-based analysis
Captions:
Revenue by Region: Total and average revenue across regions like Asia, U.K., and U.S.A.
Product Ranking by Revenue: Product sales performance ranked by revenue.
Quarterly Revenue Breakdown: Revenue grouped by quarter and country.
Product Count by Rank: Sales ranking by product category.
Yearly Revenue Trends: Performance across different financial years.
